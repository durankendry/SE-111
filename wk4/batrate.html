★★★★★ - The Batman is a cinematic masterpiece that breathes new life into one of the most iconic superheroes of all time. Directed by Matt Reeves, this film moves away from the typical origin story and instead delivers a gritty, grounded noir detective thriller that feels entirely unique. Robert Pattinson is phenomenal as a younger, more vulnerable Bruce Wayne, perfectly capturing the obsession and toll of being the Dark Knight. The film’s atmosphere is unmatched, with stunning cinematography and a haunting score by Michael Giacchino that makes Gotham City feel like a living, breathing character
